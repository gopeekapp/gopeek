What is GoPeek?

Your browser shouldn’t explode into 47 tabs just to check a few links.

GoPeek replaces the exhausting “open in new tab → wait → check → close tab” workflow with live interactive windows—Peeks—that open instantly and directly inside your current page.

Instead of constantly losing context, you can browse links, compare active pages side-by-side, minimize Peeks into floating bubbles, and continue navigating without interruption.

Most links are just curiosity clicks. GoPeek lets you satisfy them instantly without destroying your flow.

Instant Previewing

Hold Shift (or Alt / Ctrl) and hover any link to instantly open a live interactive browser window without leaving your current tab.

Sidebar Mode

Snap Peeks into a clean split-screen layout for deeper browsing sessions.

Multi-Peek

Run multiple windows side-by-side to compare docs, repos, articles, and references.

Bubble Minimize

Double-click the header to collapse any active GoPeek session into a floating bubble for later.

Dynamic Theme

GoPeek adapts visually to the website you are browsing for a native-feeling experience.
