Refund Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2026

Overview

We want you to be satisfied with your GoPeek Pro subscription. If you are not happy with the Pro features, you may request a refund under the conditions outlined below.

Refund Eligibility

Refunds are available for GoPeek Pro subscriptions within 14 days of the initial purchase date, provided that:

  • The refund request is made within 14 days of purchase
  • You have not violated the Terms of Service
  • The request is for your first Pro subscription (not renewals)

Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will not be granted in the following situations:

  • Requests made after the 14-day refund window
  • Subscription renewals or recurring billing cycles
  • Users who have significantly used Pro features during the trial period
  • Violations of the Terms of Service

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please email us at gopeekapp@gmail.com with the following information:

  • Your Paddle order ID or transaction reference
  • The email address used for purchase
  • Reason for the refund request

Refund requests are typically processed within 5-7 business days. Approved refunds will be issued to the original payment method.

Cancellation

You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time from your Paddle account dashboard. Cancellation will stop future billing but does not automatically generate a refund for the current billing period.

Contact

For refund-related questions, contact: gopeekapp@gmail.com
