Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

Overview

GoPeek is a browser extension that lets you preview links instantly in floating or embedded windows to improve browsing productivity and reduce tab overload.

Data Collection

GoPeek does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • No browsing history is collected
  • No user tracking or analytics
  • No personal identifiers are stored
  • No data is sent to external servers

How It Works (Local Processing)

When you preview a link, the page is loaded locally inside your browser using an embedded frame. All processing happens on your device. Your custom settings (like window size and UI preferences) are saved locally on your machine using Chrome's built-in local storage and are never synced externally.

Permissions Justification

To provide instant link previews, GoPeek requires limited browser permissions:

  • All Websites: Required to enable link preview functionality across websites the user actively browses.
  • Declarative Net Request: Required to enable embedding of websites that otherwise block iframe display using standard browser security headers (including X-Frame-Options and frame-ancestors). This is applied only to previewed links and does not monitor browsing activity.

Third-Party Websites

GoPeek securely displays third-party websites inside its preview window. We are not responsible for the content, cookies, or privacy practices of the external sites you choose to preview.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated as the extension evolves. Updates will be reflected on this page.
